Welcome to Kalasaar Interiors

Kalasaar Interior Design Company is one of the leading interior designers in Mumbai, specializing in residential interior design and commercial interior design projects. We create modern, functional, and aesthetically pleasing spaces that reflect your lifestyle and business needs.

Our USP lies in the vision of our founder, Shruti Dilip Gavankar, a renowned Bollywood production designer and art director, who brings the glamour and creativity of cinema into real‑world interiors. With expertise in luxury home interiors, office interiors, turnkey interior solutions, and customized furniture design, Kalasaar blends artistry with practicality to deliver spaces that are both beautiful and efficient.

Whether you are searching for top interior design firms in Mumbai, best home interior designers, or commercial interior decorators, Kalasaar offers the perfect balance of creativity, professionalism, and trust.
